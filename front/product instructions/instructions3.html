Checkme Blood Pressure Monitoring System

Reference the diagram above for proper cuff placement and measurement posture before starting. [Image of proper blood pressure cuff placement on upper arm]

Section 1: Preparation and Posture

  1. Prepare the Device: Ensure the device has fresh batteries or is connected to power. Press the **Power Button** to turn it on.
  2. Prepare Patient/Self: Sit **calmly** and quietly for at least **5 minutes** before measurement. Support your back and keep your feet flat on the floor.
  3. Prepare Arm: Remove any tight clothing from your **upper arm**. Rest your arm on a table so the cuff is at the **same level as your heart**.

Section 2: Cuff Placement and Start

  1. **Apply Cuff:** Wrap the cuff smoothly around your upper arm, making sure the **artery mark** (usually an arrow) is centered over the main artery on the inside of your arm. The bottom edge of the cuff should be about 1 inch (2-3 cm) above the elbow.
  2. **Secure Cuff:** Fasten the cuff snugly so that only one fingertip fits between the cuff and your arm.
  3. **Select User:** If the device supports multiple users, select your profile (e.g., User 1 or User 2).

Section 3: Measurement and Results

  1. **Start Measurement:** Press the large **'Start'** or **'Measure'** button. The cuff will automatically inflate. Remain still and silent during inflation and deflation.
  2. **Wait for Result:** The device will automatically deflate and display your **Systolic** (top number), **Diastolic** (bottom number), and **Pulse Rate**.
  3. **Review Trace/Data:** If applicable, check the display for any error codes. The result is automatically saved to the device memory.
  4. **Remove Cuff:** Once the measurement is complete, press the **Power Button** to turn the device off and remove the cuff.
**Safety Note:** This device is for **self-monitoring** and informational use. **It is not a substitute for a medical diagnosis.** Always consult with your healthcare professional regarding your blood pressure results and treatment plan. Wait at least 3 minutes between repeated measurements.
