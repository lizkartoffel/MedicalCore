Digital Thermometer Gun: Touchless Infrared Forehead Thermometer

Reference the diagram above for proper distance and angle before starting.

Section 1: Setup and Mode Selection

  1. Prepare the Device: Ensure the thermometer has fresh batteries. Turn the device on. If the device has different modes (e.g., body, surface, or room), select the "**Body**" or "**Forehead**" mode for accurate temperature readings.
  2. Wait for Ready: The display should show the ready symbol (often a flashing or stable unit of measure like °C or °F) before proceeding.

Section 2: Positioning and Measurement

  1. **Position the Thermometer:** Hold the thermometer gun straight and **perpendicular** to the patient's forehead. Position the sensor **1 to 2 inches (2-5 cm)** away from the center of the forehead. The sensor must not touch the skin or any hair.
  2. **Clear the Area:** Ensure the forehead is **clean, dry**, and free of sweat or hair for the most accurate reading.

Section 3: Reading and Storage

  1. **Take the Measurement:** Press and quickly release the measurement button (often a trigger). You may hear a beep or see the screen flash when the reading is complete, usually within one second.
  2. **View Result:** The temperature result will display immediately on the screen.
  3. **Store Data:** If your model has a memory function, the reading is automatically saved. Turn the thermometer off to conserve battery power.

Section 4: Maintenance

  1. **Clean Sensor:** Carefully clean the sensor lens with a **cotton swab or soft cloth** slightly moistened with alcohol. Never submerge the device in water.
  2. **Store Properly:** Keep the thermometer in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures.
Safety Note: For consistent results, ensure the thermometer has been in the room where the measurement is taken for at least **30 minutes**. Consult with a healthcare professional regarding any temperature reading that indicates a fever.
